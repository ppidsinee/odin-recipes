Pad Kra Prao

Pad Kra Prao or Chilli Basil is a thai speciality dish that you can find anywhere in Thailand. It has a spicy and savoury flavors. You can add sunny side up egg on top to reduce the spice and making the dish more balanced

Ingredients

  • Thai Basil
  • Pork mince
  • Thai chilli
  • Oyster sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Egg
  • Jasmin rice
  • Cooking oil (I prefer olive oil)
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Cook your jasmin rice
  2. Make sunny side up eggs
  3. In a pan add cooking oil and chopped garlic and cook the garlic until golden
  4. Add fresh chilli into the pad and cook for 1 minutes. You will start sneezing now that's mean you are doing it correctly
  5. Add pork mince into the pan and stir fried to mix with the chilli and garlic
  6. Add salt, pepper, oyster sauce into the pan
  7. Turn of the heat and add fresh basil. Mix the basil well with the pork
  8. Plating the dish by adding the rice first, then the Pad Kra Prao and add the sunny side egg on top
