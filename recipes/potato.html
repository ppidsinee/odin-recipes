Crunchy Potatoes

Crunchy potatoes that you can make at home, perfect to eat as a side dish

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Duck fat
  • salt
  • Pepper
  • Baking soda

Instructions

  1. Cut potatoes into square pieces
  2. Boil water in a pot
  3. Once water is hot add 1 tbsp f baking soda and add potatoes into the pot. Cook for 15 minutes
  4. Drain water from the potato but keep the potato in the pot, add duck fat, salt and pepper and toss to mix
  5. Put the potatoes on a baking sheet and cook for 20 minutes in a 195C oven
